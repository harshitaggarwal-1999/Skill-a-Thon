Vriddhi is a web application to help women in the workforce by increasing their visibility to potential investors and employers and providing them a platform to validate their skill-sets. We provide a platform for budding women entrepreneurs or women-led initiatives to meet potential investors according to their products and vice-versa. The budding entrepreneurs are also be able to register their startup/idea can also list if they need to recruit people with their required skill sets, and we will accordingly curate job opportunities for the user. We also have quizzes on different essential skill sets across different fields and award badges to job seekers.
